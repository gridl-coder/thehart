Welcome to
The White Hart

The White Hart Inn is one of Bodmin’s oldest pubs,
full of character and local stories.

Set in a listed building on Pool Street, it’s the kind of place that feels familiar the moment you walk in – low beams, slate roof, a proper bar and a friendly crowd.
